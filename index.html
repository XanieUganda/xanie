Travel to East Africa

East Africa Has 6 countries and they each have something special to offer

Kenya
kenya is the most developed country in East Africa
It has a good road network
Well protected game reserves and may wild animals to see
Nairobi its capital is very beautiful too

Uganda
Uganda has over 51 tribes
diverse cultures and languages
wildlife and waterbodies that are great for tourists

Tanzania
Tanzania is the biggest country in the region
it has the biggest game reserves and wild zebras too
The whole nation speaks one language, Swahili

Rwanda and Burundi These 2 are the smallest countries in East Africa
They have the same tribes, Hutu and Tutsi
whereas Rwanda is on a speedy development, Burundi seems stagnant

South Sudan This is the youngest country in the world
It has the tallest and darkest skinned people from Africa
